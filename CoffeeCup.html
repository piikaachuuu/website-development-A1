Coffee Cup

Pattern Steps

  • Round 1 : 6 Single crochet (SC) in a magic ring
  • Round 2 : 6 Increase (Inc) [12 Total stitches]
  • Round 3 : Single crochet (SC), Increase (Inc) [18 Total stitches]
  • Round 4 : Single crochet (SC) (BLO) [18 Total stitches]
  • Round 5-6 : Single crochet (SC) [18 Total stitches]
  • CHANGE COLOR
  • Round 7-8 : Single crochet (SC) [18 Total stitches]
  • Round 9 : 5 Single crochet (SC), Increase (Inc) [21 Total stitches]
  • Round 10 : Single crochet (SC) [21 Total stitches]
  • CHANGE COLOR
  • Round 11 : Single crochet (SC) [21 Total stitches]
  • Round 12 : 6 Single crochet (SC), Increase (Inc) [24 Total stitches]
  • CHANGE COLOR
  • Round 13 : Single crochet (SC) [24 Total stitches]
  • Round 14 : Single crochet (SC) (BLO) [24 Total stitches]
  • Round 15 : 2 Single, Decrease (Dec) (BLO) [18 Total stitches]
  • Round 16 : Single crochet (SC), Decrease (Dec) [12 Total stitches]
  • Round 17 : Decrease (Dec) [6 Total stitches]
  • SEWING

Tips & Materials

  • Yarn : Milk cotton (your choice of color)
  • Hook : 2.5mm
  • Stuffing : polyester fiberfill
  • Safety eyes : 6mm
  • Crochet Marker
  • Needle
  • Scissor

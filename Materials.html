What materials that we need for crocheting?

1. Crochet Hook

A crochet hook is the most important tool you need to start crocheting. Its's used to pull the yarn through loops and create stitches. Crochet hooks come in many sizes and materials, and the right size depends on the yarn you are using. If you're a beginner, a medium-sized hook is the easiest to learn with.

2. Yarn

Yarn is important for making crochet items. There are many types of yarn, depending on color, thickness, and texture. Choose the yarn based on your project or personal choice.

3. Marker

A stitch marker helps you keep track of your stitches or rounds while crocheting. It marks your place so you don't get confused or lose your starting point

4. Stuffing

Stuffing is used for projects where you need to fill the inside, such as dolls or plush toys (amigurumi).

5. Safety Eyes

These are used for making eyes for amigurumi or crochet animals. There are different sizes, depending on the design.

6. Needles

Needles are needed for sewing pieces together or weaving in yarn ends. They come in different sizes, depending on what you need to do.

7. Scissor

Scissor are essential in crochet because they allow you to cut yarn cleanly and neatly, helping you achieve a tidy and professional finish for your projects

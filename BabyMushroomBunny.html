Baby Mushroom Bunny

Pattern Steps


    HAT
  • Round 1 : 6 Single crochet (SC) in a magic ring
  • Round 2 : 6 Increase (Inc) [12 Total stitches]
  • Round 3 : Single crochet (SC), Increase (Inc) [18 Total stitches]
  • Round 4 : Single crochet (SC), Increase (Inc), Single crochet (SC) [24 Total stitches]
  • Round 5-6 : 24 Single crochet (SC) [24 Total stitches]
  • Round 7 : 3 Single crochet (SC), 6 Increase (Inc) (FLO) [30 Total stiches]
  • Round 8 : 2 Single crochet, Increase (Inc), 2 Single crochet [36 Total stiches]

    • BODY
  • Round 9 : 24 Single crochet (SC) (BLO) [24 Total stitches]
  • Round 10-11 : 24 Single crochet (SC) [24 Total stitches]
  • Round 12 : 7 Single crochet (SC), BO, 8 Single crochet (SC), BO, 7 Single crochet
  • Round 13-14 : 24 Single crochet (SC) [24 stitches]
  • Round 15 : 9 Single crochet (SC), BO, 5 Single crochet (SC), BO, 8 Single crochet (SC)
  • Round 16 : 12 Decrease (DEC)

    • STUFFING AND ADD EYE
  • Round 17 : 6 Decrease (Dec)

    • EAR
  • Round 1 : 6 Single crochet (SC) in a magic ring
  • Round 2 : Single crochet (SC), Increase (Inc) [3 Times]
  • Round 3-4 : 9 Single crochet [9 Total stitches]
  • Round 5 : Single crochet (SC), Decrease (Dec) [6 Total stitches]

    • CUT AND SEWING

Tips & Materials

  • Yarn : Milk cotton (your choice of color)
  • Hook : 2.5mm
  • Stuffing : polyester fiberfill
  • Safety eyes : 6mm
  • Crochet Marker
  • Needle
  • Scissor

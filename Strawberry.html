Strawberry

Pattern Steps


    STRAWBERRY
  • Round 1 : 6 Single crochet (SC) in a magic ring
  • Round 2 : Single crochet, Increase (Inc) [3 Times]
  • Round 3 : 2 Single crochet (SC), Increase (Inc) [3 Times]
  • Round 4 : 3 Single crochet (SC), Increase (Inc) [3 Times]
  • Round 5 : 4 Single crochet (SC), Increase (Inc) [3 Times]
  • Round 6 : 2 Single crochet (SC), Increase (Inc) [6 Times]
  • Round 7-8 : Single crochet [24 Total stiches]
  • Round 9 : 2 Single crochet (SC), Decrease (Dec) [6 Times]
  • Round 10 : Single crochet (SC), Decrease (Dec) [6 Times]

    • STUFFING
  • Round 11 : 6 Decrease (Dec) [6 Times]

    • SEWING

Tips & Materials

  • Yarn : Milk cotton (your choice of color)
  • Hook : 2.5mm
  • Stuffing : polyester fiberfill
  • Crochet Marker
  • Needle
  • Scissor
